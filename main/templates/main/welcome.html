Help Us Understand Diabetes Care

We are creating a community of patients, providers, designers and developers to ease the care and management of type 1 diabetes. You can find out more about who we are here.

The first step to creating a better care experience is identifying where there are negative experiences, breakdowns, and problems. Once we can visualize our communities issues, we will prototype new experiences that ease caring for diabetes.

Help us by participating in our process. Share your experiences and opinions with our community.

We are going to use this information to inform the next steps of developing a community to help patients, caregivers, and providers develop solutions to ease the care and management of type 1 diabetes.
